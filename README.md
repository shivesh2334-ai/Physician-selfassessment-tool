# Physician Professional Self-Assessment Tool

A Streamlit-based application for physicians to assess their patient-centered care skills across four critical dimensions of trust and connection.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Assessment**: 20 questions across 4 categories
- **Weighted Scoring**: Questions are weighted based on importance
- **Interactive Visualizations**: Bar charts and radar charts
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Actionable improvement suggestions
- **Multiple Export Formats**: JSON and Excel report downloads
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices

## Categories Assessed

1. **Personal Connect (Do You Care About Me?)**
   - Patient naming and personal contact
   - Sitting during consultations
   - Follow-up calls
   - Empathy and active listening
   - Personal life discussions

2. **Trust of Your Trade (Are You the Best?)**
   - Continuing education attendance
   - Research reading habits
   - Skill development
   - Confidence in knowledge gaps
   - Excellence pursuit

3. **Social Trust (Can I Trust You?)**
   - Cross-cultural trust building
   - Safe environment creation
   - Reliability in follow-up
   - Demonstrated care
   - Encouraging sensitive sharing

4. **Treating Style (Are You Treating Me Differently?)**
   - Health disparities awareness
   - Bias examination
   - Equitable treatment
   - Social determinants knowledge
   - Self-reflection on judgment
